Glitch Images: The Twisted Joys of Amusement Parks

I decided to use my three favorite amusement parks as the topic of my three glitch edits. I chose these amusement parks to play around with the idea that the amusement parks that bring me joy, thrill, and happiness are actually creepy, dark, and twisted places filled with secrets. There have been many stories and rumors about amusement parks and their grim events such as deceased visitors who are now spirits and remain at the parks, the dumping of loved one's ashes on rides, secret underground tunneling systems, and more.
